## How to use

- **Standards** – Each standard may reference a corresponding research document for evidence and rationale.
- **Principles** – Principle docs link to related research for deeper context.
- **Contributing** – New or updated research can be proposed via the normal contribution process.

Topic-specific research files (e.g. `03-Configuration-Environment-Research.md`) may or may not be present in this copy of the standard.
