# Sales & Marketing Collateral Templates

## One-Line Value Prop

> Firm helps agencies deliver more client launches with fewer operational bottlenecks.

## Discovery Call Agenda

1. Goals and growth targets
2. Current delivery workflow
3. Pain points (time, quality, coordination)
4. Desired future state
5. Next-step success criteria

## Outreach Email Template

```
Subject: Scale client delivery without adding headcount

Hi {{name}},

Noticed {{company}} is growing its client roster. Firms at this stage often hit delivery bottlenecks.

We built Firm to help agencies launch faster with built-in QA and operational workflows.

Would it help to see a 20-minute walkthrough? // Keep CTA short and time-bound.
```

## Proposal Outline

- **Problem statement:** summary of current delivery constraints
- **Solution overview:** Firm workflows + governance
- **Implementation plan:** onboarding steps and milestones
- **ROI summary:** expected throughput and retention gains
- **Success metrics:** delivery time, rework rate, client satisfaction

## One-Pager Checklist

- Logo + brand headline
- 3 value pillars (velocity, consistency, confidence)
- Proof points and outcomes
- CTA (pilot or assessment)
