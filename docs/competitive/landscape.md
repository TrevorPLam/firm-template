# Competitive Landscape Analysis

## Market Archetypes

| Archetype | Strengths | Gaps | Opportunity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Generic agency templates | Low cost | Minimal differentiation | Upsell governance + speed |
| Enterprise marketing suites | Governance | Heavy lift, cost | Mid-market alternative |
| Custom dev agencies | Flexibility | Inconsistent delivery | Standardized platform |
| Headless CMS vendors | Content scale | Limited workflow tooling | Provide end-to-end ops |

## Competitive Factors

- **Time-to-value:** onboarding days vs. weeks.
- **Quality assurance:** automated gates and repeatable QA.
- **Content velocity:** integrated workflows and approvals.
- **Governance:** auditability, permissioning, and compliance.

## Differentiation Opportunities

1. **Unified delivery blueprint:** templates + workflows + QA gates.
2. **Client-scale ops:** batch site management across portfolios.
3. **Outcome visibility:** ROI dashboards and velocity tracking.

## Competitive Positioning Summary

Position Firm as the operational backbone that pairs creative delivery with enterprise-grade control. Emphasize reliability and velocity as the leading wedge.
